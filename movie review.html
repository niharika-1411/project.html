Movie Review

Guntur Kaaram

Movie Details

  • Director: Trivikram Srinivas
  • Lead Actor: Mahesh Babu
  • Other Key Cast: Sreeleela, Prakash Raj, Ramya Krishna, Meenakshi Chaudhary, Jagapathi Babu, Jayaram, Sunil, Rao Ramesh
  • Production: Haarika & Hassine Creations
  • Music: Thaman S

Story Summary

  • Ramana (Mahesh Babu) is raised in Guntur, managing the family's chili business, after his mother, Vyra Vasundhara (Ramya Krishna), leaves him as a child.
  • He is later summoned to Hyderabad to sign papers that would sever ties with his mother, who is now running for office and is pressured by her influential grandfather.
  • The film explores Ramana's journey to understand his mother's abandonment and his struggle to reconcile with her.
  • It also delves into the family and business feuds that lead to a confrontation between Ramana and his grandfather.

Movie Highlights

  • "Guntur Kaaram" is a mixed bag, with Mahesh Babu's performance being a major highlight but the film ultimately struggling with a weak screenplay and execution.
  • Mahesh Babu's charisma, dialogue delivery, and energetic portrayal of Ramana are consistently praised.
  • The emotional scenes between Ramana and his mother, played by Ramya Krishna, are considered impactful by some.

Movie Rating

4.4
